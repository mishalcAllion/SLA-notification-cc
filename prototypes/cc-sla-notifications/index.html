SLA Notifications & Unreads

Timer-based SLA tracking for Command Center messaging. Tracks elapsed time since last inbound client message, surfaces Green/Yellow/Red status on thread cards, sorts by urgency, and provides management config + dashboards. 6 features across 3 prototype screens.
